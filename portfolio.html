Aisha Riley



I was born and raised in Owensboro, Kentucky under my parents, Katie Riley and James Louis McCarley. As a free-spirited little girl, I faced some challenges of my own. At the age of three, I had to learn how to function in this world while battling Level I Autism. Yes, it was tough living in a household of nine (four sisters, two brothers, and of course including myself). I studied the violin at the age of ten and that has changed my life. I graduated from high school with honors on May 25, 1993 and attended Eastern Kentucky University in August of 1993. In 1998, I attended Owensboro Community and Technical College and graduated in May 2000. Then, I attended Eastern Kentucky University off and on until I finally graduated with an Associates of Applied Science Degree in Computer Electronics Technology in December 2009. I would reside in Louisville, Kentucky in May 2010 working as a Help Desk Specialist. Finally, in 2018, I returned back to Owensboro, Kentucky to care for my mother and myself until my mother's passing in May 27, 2021 from COVID-19. Currently, I will be graduating in May with an Associates Degree in Computer Information Techology - Programming and also receiving five other certificates. Being unsure of my life's direction, my main focus is to pick up the pieces and live my life to the fullest.
